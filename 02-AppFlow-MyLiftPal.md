**Flow Steps:**

1. **Landing Page**
   - See app value proposition
   - Options: Sign In, Sign Up

2. **Sign Up Form**
   - Enter: Email, Password, Display Name
   - Accept terms
   - Submit

3. **Email Verification**
   - Check email for verification link
   - Click link to verify

4. **Welcome Screen**
   - Brief explanation of app
   - Set preferences (weight unit, rest time)
   - CTA: "Create Your First Training Block"

5. **Create First Training Block**
   - Guided wizard flow (see Flow 3.2)
